Here's a time line of Kurt Cobain's life:
  • February 20, 1967 Cobain was born at Grays Harbor Hospital in Aberdeen, Washington, the son of waitress Wendy Elizabeth (née Fradenburg; born 1948) and automotive mechanic Donald Leland Cobain (born 1946).
  • 1976 His parents divorced. He later said that the divorce had a profound effect on his life.
  • June 28, 1979 Cobain's mother granted full custody to his father.
  • February 20, 1981 Cobain's uncle offered him either a bike or a used guitar—he chose the guitar
  • During high school Cobain rarely found anyone with whom he could play music. While hanging out at the Melvins' practice space, he met Novoselic, a fellow devotee of punk rock.
  • 1987 Novoselic finally agreed to join Cobain, forming the beginnings of Nirvana.
  • 1989 Nirvana recorded the album Bleach, released on Sub Pop Records
  • 1991 With Grohl, the band found their greatest success through their major-label debut, Nevermind. With Nevermind's lead single, "Smells Like Teen Spirit", Nirvana entered the mainstream, popularizing a subgenre of alternative rock called "grunge". Since their debut, Nirvana has sold over 25 million albums in the United States (U.S.) alone, and over 75 million worldwide.
  • February 24, 1992 Cobain and Courtney Love were married on Waikiki Beach in Hawaii
  • March 18, 1994 Love phoned the Seattle police informing them that Cobain was suicidal and had locked himself in a room with a gun. Police arrived and confiscated several guns and a bottle of pills from Cobain, who insisted that he was not suicidal and had locked himself in the room to hide from Love.

I think that he committed suicide. I don't think there's a smoking gun. And I think there's only one way you can explain a lot of things around his death. Not that he was murdered, but that there was just a lack of caring for him. I just think that Courtney had moved on, and he was expendable.

In a 1998 interview Nick Broomfield
